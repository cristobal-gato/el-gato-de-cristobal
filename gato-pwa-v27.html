📲 Get the App on Your Phone

📱 iPhone / iPad
1. Open this link in Safari — it won't work from Chrome or Mail
2. Tap the Share button ⎋ at the bottom of the screen
3. Scroll down in the menu and tap "Add to Home Screen"
4. Tap Add in the top right — done! The app icon appears on your home screen
💡 Once installed, it works like a real app — even offline
🤖 Android
1. Open this link in Chrome
2. Tap the ⋮ menu in the top right corner
3. Tap "Add to Home screen"
4. Tap Add — the app icon appears on your home screen
💡 Must be opened in Chrome for this to work
